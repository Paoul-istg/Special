Happy Monthsary, my beautiful Babi ❤️ Of all the lives I could be living right now, I'm happy that I exist in yours. Eleven months with you babi have been one of the best parts of my life. I may not be a perfect man, but I want you to know that even if I have to walk through heat, even if I have to limit myself from spending just to buy you things that you want, even if I have to go home late at night just to see you—none of it would ever feel like a burden because that's how much I love you. In all of this time, one thing has stayed the same for me: I love you at your best, and I will never leave you at your worst. I love you my hamster.
